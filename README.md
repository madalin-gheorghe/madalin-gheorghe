# Mădălin Gheorghe
Computational Designer & Design Technologist working across generative design, additive manufacturing, custom toolpaths, and fabrication-ready computational systems.
I build workflows that translate complex geometry into physical outcomes: Grasshopper/C# tools, Processing-based simulations, custom G-code, large-format 3D printing strategies and material-driven design systems.
My work connects architecture, product design and manufacturing, with projects ranging from architectural-scale robotic fabrication to award-winning lighting and product design projects.

## Focus
- Computational geometry
- Rhino / Grasshopper workflows
- C# scripting and tool development
- Custom G-code and toolpath generation
- Large-format additive manufacturing
- Ceramic and polymer 3D printing
- Material behaviour and fabrication constraints
- Design-for-manufacturing

### Nuclei
Grasshopper plugin for bottom-up generative systems, behaviour-based simulations and voxel-controlled spatial maps.

### Cloud Pergola
Computational design contribution for the Croatian National Pavilion at the Venice Architecture Biennale 2018, using vector fields, agent-based behaviour, voxel rationalisation and robotic fabrication constraints.

### Custom G-code workflows
Toolpath logic for non-standard additive manufacturing setups, including extrusion control, continuous paths, non-planar deposition and fabrication-aware material distribution.
